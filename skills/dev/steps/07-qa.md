## Goal

Verify the current integrated UI against acceptance criteria and realistic edge states, then record a current QA gate without loading code-review or ship instructions.

## How

1. Confirm current HEAD, acceptance criteria, affected routes, platform, and the Design Critique artifact. Use Quick, Focused, or Full depth from the durable route; do not recompute safety from task kind alone.
2. Read and execute `qa.md`. Run assertion-driven checks for primary, empty, boundary, error, navigation, responsive, and accessibility behavior that applies to the change.
3. An unavailable app, auth flow, database, seed, or required service is `blocked`, never `passed` or `skipped`. Preserve the exact environment failure and smallest remediation.
4. Fix Critical/High and core-flow Medium findings. Re-run the affected assertions and smoke-check adjacent routes. Bound the fix/recheck loop to three attempts.
5. Save the QA report outside canonical JSON. Update the gate manifest with `qa: passed` at current HEAD and the report path. A failed or blocked verdict must not create a passing row.
6. Return a strict phase result with current commit and passing `test` evidence pointing to the QA report. Record it through the runner; do not add Markdown-like QA sections to `session.json`.

## Done-when

- Applicable acceptance criteria and edge states have executed assertions.
- The final verdict is Pass or Pass with concerns, with no unresolved blocking finding.
- QA gate and phase evidence are current for HEAD and validate.

**Advance:** record the result and proceed to Step 08 (Review), as selected by the runner.
