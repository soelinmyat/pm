## Goal

Present the reviewed proposal in a legible reader whose visible decision, lifecycle, evidence, and open-question markers match canonical data.

## How

Run artifact validation and browser/render checks at representative desktop, tablet, narrow, and print surfaces. When the proposal template changed materially, run current `pm:design-critique` and retain its report/manifest. Fix the generator or template, regenerate, and recertify the exact proposal; never repair one output manually.

Show the HTML reader and summarize the recommendation, smallest scope, biggest risk, and decisions still requiring the user.

## Done-when

The human reader is verified at required surfaces and accurately exposes the exact reviewed proposal and approval boundary.

**Advance:** proceed to Step 9 (Approval).
