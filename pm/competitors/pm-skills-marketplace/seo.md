## Content Strategy Signals

- **Blog cadence:** The Product Compass publishes weekly. PM Skills Marketplace was launched with a single, comprehensive blog post -- not a drip campaign. Future content about the marketplace will likely be woven into regular newsletter issues.
- **SEO plays:** No deliberate SEO strategy for the tool itself. Distribution is entirely audience-driven (newsletter -> social -> GitHub stars -> trending). The creator explicitly rejected sponsor opportunities and podcast invitations to maintain focus.
- **Content gaps:** No comparison pages, no landing pages, no SEO-optimized documentation site. The README is the only documentation. This is deliberate -- the product is free and open-source with no monetization, so SEO investment would have no ROI.
- **Distribution moat:** The real "SEO" for PM Skills Marketplace is Huryn's 128K+ newsletter audience and social media following. This is a creator-led distribution model, not a search-driven one. Replicating the product is easy (MIT license); replicating the distribution is hard.
