How It Works

Your product lifecycle, structured

Every session builds on the last. Knowledge compounds over time.

1

Start with an idea

Run /pm:groom with your idea. PM researches the market, checks competitors, and scopes the work.

2

Build with context

Groomed issues flow into dev with zero handoff. Research and scope carry through to implementation.

3

Ship with confidence

Review gates, TDD, CI monitoring, auto-merge. From groomed issue to merged PR in one session.
